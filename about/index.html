About Me

For the past 10 years, I have worked with full-stack development in several startups and companies, mostly revolving around Ruby on Rails. Latest as a senior software engineer at Peanut, building APIs for scaling a social network for women. Before that as CTO and co-founder building a platform for ordering and delivering graphic designs.

My drive comes from delivering value to the business, and I love being part of solving problems from initial ideas to deployment. Besides having solid experience in building web-applications, I also have experience with marketing automation, finance, analytics, mobile development and leading small teams.

You can read more about me on LinkedIn or my resume.

If you have any ideas or projects that you think could benefit from my skills and knowledge, please don't hesitate to send me a message or connect via Twitter.
