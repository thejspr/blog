About Me

For the past 7 years I have worked as a developer in several startups, working with various technologies like Ruby on Rails, React and Android. Throughout this time I have evolved my skills in building features that focus on solving the problem in the simplest and fastest way possible. I believe that getting a feature out in front of the user and iterating on it based on their feedback and behaviour, is the most important aspect of whether it will be a success.

I love being involved in the creative process from idea conception to deploying and maintaining the finished product, and this is one of the reasons I decided to become a self-employed software consultant in 2015. I am well-versed in both front-end and back-end development, not shying away from anything that is needed to get the job done. Whilst I enjoy working with the latest and greatest frameworks and languages, I prefer using stable technologies that have been tested and honed.

In summery, the values that drive my work are:

  • Communication is paramount to the success of a project.
  • A problem is best solved in iterations guided by a solid feedback loop.
  • Test-Driven Development helps ensure the correctness and maintainability of a system.

You can read more about me on LinkedIn, my resume and the projects page.

If you have any ideas or projects that you think could benefit from my skills and knowledge, please don't hesitate to send me a message or connect via Twitter.
