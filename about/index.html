About Me

For the past 3 years I have worked as a developer in two startups, both revolving around Ruby on Rails applications. Throughout this time I have evolved my skills in building features that focus on solving the problem in the simplest and fastest way possible. I believe that getting a feature out in front of the user and iterating on it based on their feedback and behaviour, is the most important aspect of whether it will be a success.

I love being involved in the creative process from idea conception to deploying and maintaining the finished product, and this is one of the reasons I have decided to become self-employed as a freelancer in 2015. I am well-versed in both front-end and back-end development, not shying away from anything that needs doing to get the job done. Whilst I enjoy working with the latest and greatest frameworks and languages, I prefer using stable technologies with sizeable communities and usage.

In summery, the values that drive my work are;

  • A problem is best solved in iterations guided by a solid feedback loop.
  • TDD helps ensure the correctness and maintainability of a system.
  • Communication is paramount to the success of a project.

You can read more about me on LinkedIn, my resume and the projects page.

If you have any ideas or projects that you think could benefit from my skills and knowledge, or just want to have a chat, please don't hesitate to send me a message or connect via Twitter.
